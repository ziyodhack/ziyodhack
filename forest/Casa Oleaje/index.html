Modern Jungle Living in Santa Teresa's Premier Eco-Community

Set within the lush, peaceful hills just a short 7-minute drive from the vibrant surf town of Santa Teresa, this brand-new tropical villa offers a lifestyle of serenity, design, and connection to nature.

With 127m² of interior space on a generous 900m² lot, this 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom residence features soaring ceilings, natural wood details, and expansive glass doors that open to a sun-drenched terrace and private 27m² pool framed by palm trees and tropical landscaping.

Crafted with a tropical-modern aesthetic, every design detail celebrates simplicity and elegance—perfect for a personal retreat, rental investment, or full-time home. Still under construction, the home offers buyers the opportunity to personalize finishes.

About Organica Eco-Community

This home is part of Organica, a eco-barrio near Santa Teresa built on the principles of permaculture, agroforestry, and organic architecture. More than 15,000 trees and fruit trees—including multiple varieties of mango, banana, and other native species—have been thoughtfully planted to regenerate the land and create a thriving food forest.

Permaculture Design

The community is designed following permaculture principles, creating a sustainable ecosystem that works with nature rather than against it.

Food Forest

With over 15,000 trees planted, including fruit-bearing varieties, residents enjoy access to organic produce grown right in the community.

Organic Architecture

Homes in Organica are designed to blend with the natural environment, using sustainable materials and energy-efficient designs.
